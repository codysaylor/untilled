Two & Two Together

By Brooke Herbert Hayes

After struggling for more than a year with health issues, 18-year old Zoe Wolfe was finally diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes just before Christmas 2014. She lives with her dad, Todd, and younger sister, Faith, on former farmland that has been in their family for seven generations. After the family decided to stop farming the land, they began to take in foster children and to rely on the local food pantry for groceries.
